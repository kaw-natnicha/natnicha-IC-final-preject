Festival Activities

  • Live performances are one of the easiest ways to feel the atmosphere of a festival.

    • Watch dance groups, musicians, and local performers throughout the event schedule.

    • Arrive early for stage events if you want a clear view.

    • Check whether photography is allowed before recording close-up performances.

  • Food stalls make many events feel like a full day trip, not just a quick stop.

    • Try local festival snacks, seasonal drinks, and international food booths.

    • Bring some cash because small vendors may not accept cards.

    • Look for trash stations and follow the event's sorting rules.

  • Evening events can be the most memorable, especially lanterns, floats, fireworks, and light shows.

    • Bring a portable charger if you plan to stay from afternoon through night.

    • Choose a nearby station exit before the crowd gets heavy.

    • Give yourself extra time to leave after fireworks or major parades.
