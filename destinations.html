Japan Destinations

  • KYOTO FESTIVAL DISTRICT

    Kyoto is one of Japan's best places for traditional events, shrine processions, old streets, and seasonal celebrations. It is the main destination for Gion Matsuri and many smaller cultural festivals.

  • OSAKA RIVER AND SHRINE AREA

    Osaka brings a lively city atmosphere to traditional celebration. Tenjin Matsuri is known for shrine rituals, river boats, street energy, and evening fireworks along the Okawa River.

  • AOMORI SUMMER FESTIVAL ROUTE

    Aomori is a northern highlight for summer travel. Nebuta Matsuri fills the streets with huge illuminated floats, drums, dancers, and a bright nighttime festival mood.

  • TOKYO INTERNATIONAL EVENT AREAS

    Tokyo is useful for international festivals, exhibitions, food fairs, music events, and pop-up cultural weekends. Yoyogi Park, Shiba Park, and major galleries are good places to check first.
